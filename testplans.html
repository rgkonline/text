Help

Test Plans provide TES with an overall view of the Test Schedule. View our Guide to Test Plans before submitting a Test Plan.



Plan Duration

A single test plan can be for a maximum of 31 days in duration, and can be submitted upto 14 days in advance of the plan starting.



Planned Maintenance

TES have a planned maintenance window every Sunday between 6pm and midnight, UK Time. Please arrange for all testing to be conducted outside of this window to avoid potential impacts to planned testing.



Out-Of-Hours Testing

It is essential to inform TES of all testing which is taking place Out Of Standard UK Working Hours. By understanding Out-Of-Hours testing, we can plan outages with a high degree of confidence in terms of impact to our customers, or inform you when there are incidents which may impact your planned testing.



Mainframe Testing

It is essential to inform TES of all Mainframe based testing so that demand is understood on a 24x7 basis. In all cases, you are required to supply the estimated volume of MIPS that will be used during the period of testing.



Approvals

Test Plans are automatically approved presently. In the coming months, demand for Mainframe based testing will be balanced against supply, and plans will be approved or rejected on this basis.
